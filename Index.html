WELCOME TO HAMI NEPALI

The place wherever we stay, the place where we live, the place where we go, do what we want.

The main objective of the organization is to serve the working Nepalis materially, socially, financially and humanely with service, harmony and a pure heart. Inspire the Global unification for social change of Nepalese. Let's begin our journey to change the perception of people together.

Our Objectives

  • To run it as a non profit, accountable, responsible, transparent and people's social walfare organization.
  • To unify the globally scattered Nepalese and transmit the knowledge and expertise for nepal and Nepalese through conductin research, meating and confrence.
  • To lunch creative activies for insurring the rights of all classes, origins, religious groups, different cast and ethinic groups.
  • To conduct training activites for empowering and developing capacites in health, education, sports, water management , agriculture and animal husbandry.
  • Advocate for marginalized community, minorites, children and women's rights, gender equality, gender based voilence, health, women's empowerment and progess to education, public awerness campaings, good governance, networking, lobbying, training, workshop, literacy, education and widening opportutines for employment.
  • To provide serices for the people and communities that is affected by natural disasters, natural calamities, and different kinds of emergency incidents.
  • To conserve, protect and promote, the Nepalese arts,culture and ingeunity.
  • To take permission from relevent bodies before starting and operating any program activies.
